A 90-day map so you always know what to do next.

The loop itself is simple. Where people stall is trying to do all four steps at once.

So we spread it across 90 days. One focus at a time. In an order that actually makes sense. In time blocks that fit a real schedule.

When you sit down to work, you're not asking "what should I do?" You're asking "what does the plan say for this week?"

How the 90 days break down:

Weeks 1–4

Clarity and cleanup

You get your shop back under control so every decision after this gets easier. No random changes. No fixing everything at once. Just the few moves that clear the fog fast.

Weeks 5–8

Build the rhythm

This is where the loop clicks. Research. Create. List. Repeat. You stop needing motivation because you're not reinventing the process every single time you sit down.

Weeks 9–12

Improve what's already working

Instead of starting over, you adjust the right lever. Small tweaks that compound. Cleaner decisions. More momentum.

What this actually looks like day to day:

  • You wake up knowing what you're working on this week.
  • You stop burning your limited hours just deciding what to do.
  • You make progress without needing four hours a day to do it.

That handles the plan. The last piece is having someone in your corner when things get unclear.
