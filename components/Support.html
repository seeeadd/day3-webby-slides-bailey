You do not have to do this alone.

And you do not have to wait a week to get help.

This is where most Etsy plans break down.

You get motivated, you start, you hit a question on a Tuesday night, and then you lose a week because you do not know what to do next.

So the Accelerator is built around support while you implement.

Not "watch more videos." Actual help while you are working.

1
12 Weeks

Weekly Implementation + Shop Review Calls

  • 60 to 75 minutes every week for 12 weeks
  • Weeks 1 to 4: heavy live shop audits and hot seats
  • Weeks 5 to 12: troubleshooting, Q&A, and implementation focus
  • Every call ends with: here is exactly what to do this week
  • You learn from your own shop and from watching others get coached
2
90 Days

Inner Circle WhatsApp Group

Daily access to the cohort while you are in the sprint.

  • Ask questions while you are building
  • Share work in progress
  • Bailey responds with voice notes when available

This is for the moments you would normally spiral or stall.

3
24/7

Bailey Bot

When you are working and a question pops up, you do not have to stop.

  • Trained on Bailey's courses, calls, and strategies
  • Ask tactical questions anytime and get Bailey-aligned guidance while you work

Because the fastest way to quit is doing it alone and second-guessing every decision.
