Here is what your 90 days looks like in real life.

Most Etsy plans break down at the same point.

You sit down to work. You open Etsy. Then you stare at your screen for 15 minutes trying to decide:

Do I create something new? Fix an old listing? Research tags? Watch another tutorial?

By the time you decide, half your work block is gone.

We remove that failure point completely. You get a plan before you start, built around your shop and your real schedule.

1 Step

Kickoff call so you start clean

60 to 90 min live call where you meet your cohort, set your goals, and get oriented. You ask questions before the sprint starts so you are not confused on Day 1.

2 Step

Your personalized 90-day roadmap

You fill out a detailed intake form about your shop, your goals, your challenges, and how much time you actually have. Our team builds the first draft. Bailey personally reviews and approves each one. Then you receive your custom week-by-week plan.

You are not deciding what to do. You are following the week you are on.

3 Step

The 3-hour weekly system

Here is the loop you run every week:

Monday Research 1 hour

Find 3 to 5 validated product ideas. Research ends when you have the ideas.

Wednesday Create and list 90 min

Turn Monday's ideas into finished products and list them. Batch the work so you stay in flow.

Friday Optimize 30 min

Review data and improve listings. No random overhauls.

3 hours total
Weekends off. Never more than 1.5 hours at once. You get 48 hours between sessions so your brain is not constantly context-switching.

This is built for people who have jobs, kids, and 90 other things competing for their time.
